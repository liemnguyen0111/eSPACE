Our Mission Statement

What is the purpose of eSPACE?


Teach

We strive to provide an interactive learning experience that keeps you searching for more.

Make Learning Fun

Learning doesn't always have to be boring! With eSPACE, we want to show you a unique way you can keep up with the stars above.

Inspiration


Why Did We Make eSPACE?

When you live in a universe as unique and interesting as this, you can find inspiration all around you. From dying stars, to the mysteries of planetary life, to that feeling of wonder when we look at the night sky above - space helps us question ourselves and the things around us. The more answers we get, the more questions we have. We're here to help answer some of those questions as well as provide you with some new ones as well. So sit back and enjoy yourself as you enter one of the biggest and longest mysteries of all time: space!
