Apollo 11 The one thing about space we all know

The spaceflight that first landed humans on the Moon. Commander Neil Armstrong and lunar module pilot Buzz Aldrin formed the American crew that landed the Apollo Lunar Module Eagle on July 20, 1969. The fifth crewed mission of NASA's Apllo program, launched by a Saturn V rocket from Kennedy Space Center on Merritt Island, Florida.

The entirety of NASA's Apollo Space Program was about $25.4 billion, about $152 billion in today's dollars. While a lot of money were put into this program. Some technology we uses today can be traced all the way to the Apollo Program, from industrial tools used all the way to headsets used for aviation and gaming.
